Why Cyber Awareness Matters for Small Businesses

Small businesses are prime targets for cyber attacks because they often lack dedicated security teams. A single phishing email or weak password can lead to financial loss, reputational damage, and legal consequences.

Staff awareness is the first line of defence. When employees understand phishing scams, strong password practices, and social engineering tactics, they significantly reduce the risk of security breaches.

What You Will Learn

  • How to secure accounts with strong passwords
  • How social engineering exploits human behaviour
  • How small changes reduce major cyber risks
