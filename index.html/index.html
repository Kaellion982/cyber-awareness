Why Cyber Awareness Matters for Small Businesses

Small businesses are increasingly targeted by cyber criminals because they often lack dedicated IT security teams. A single phishing email, weak password, or employee mistake can lead to data breaches, financial loss, reputational damage, and legal consequences.

Cyber awareness training helps staff recognise threats before they cause harm. When employees understand phishing scams, password best practices, and social engineering tactics, they become the first line of defence against cyber attacks.

What You Will Learn

  • How to identify phishing emails and online scams
  • How to create strong passwords and protect accounts
  • How social engineering exploits human error
  • How small behavioural changes reduce major security risks
